4 Weeks Inktober Tournaments 2019

The Okegom Wikia community held 4 mini-contests, one for each full week of October, the main point was the usage of ink for physical art, (though we shared lots of digital art) based on specific horror related themes.

Week 1 - Red Sea

Week 2 - Undead and vampires

Week 3 - Pitch Black World / Witches

Week 4 - Halloween Costumes
